The Foodisco Manual

The official user-friendly guide on how to use foodisco.

About

What is foodisco?

Foodisco is the best way to find new restaurants near you. Gone are the days of indecisive friend groups taking hours to decide where to eat.

Use Foodisco to find some of the best restaurants in your area.

Features

What it can do

  • Automatically fills in your location, no need to type it in.
  • Finds the best restaurants near you.
  • Easily get directions to a restaurant.
  • Save Favorite Restaurants.
  • See restaurants you previously skipped with the skipped list view
  • Dislike a restaurant? Easily prevent that listing from showing up again.
  • Allows you to create an account or log in with an existing Google or Facebook account

Get Started

Step 1 | Enter your Location

To enter a location, either:

  • Type your location in the search box
  • Click the gps icon to the right of the search box
  • Press enter to automatically grab your location using gps

Log in Using an Account

Option 1 | Log in using a Facebook or Google Account

No need to sign up, simply log in using an existing account.

Option 2 | Sign up for an account

Fill out your email and password on the sign-up form and receive an email confirming your registration.



User Panel | View Stats

Be able to lookup your favorite and excluded restaurants. A user statistics page option will also be available in the future.
